About Adaeze

I see what others miss in transformation

Most consultants arrive with a framework and leave with a report. I arrive curious, stay present, and help you build something that actually lasts. That's the Business Therapist approach.

I'm also completing a Master's in Computer Science (AI specialisation), ensuring my practice stays grounded in where the technology is actually going.
